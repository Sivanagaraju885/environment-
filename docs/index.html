Precautions

  • Segregate waste into dry and wet waste
  • Avoid excessive plastic usage
  • Dispose hazardous waste carefully
  • Do not burn waste openly
  • Use covered bins to prevent pollution
  • Wear gloves while handling waste
  • Maintain cleanliness in surroundings

Limitations

  • Lack of public awareness
  • Improper waste segregation
  • Limited recycling facilities
  • High operational and maintenance cost
  • Insufficient government monitoring
  • Limited access to modern technology
