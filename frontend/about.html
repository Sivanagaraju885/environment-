We aim to build a cleaner and greener environment by using technology to manage waste efficiently and responsibly.

The Waste Management System is designed to promote a clean, healthy, and sustainable environment by enabling proper handling and disposal of waste. Rapid urbanization and population growth have increased waste generation, making effective waste management a critical requirement for environmental protection and public health.

This system provides a digital platform where citizens can report waste issues in their surroundings. By collecting accurate waste information such as type, quantity, and location, the system helps authorities take timely action and maintain cleanliness in urban and rural areas.
